Glossary

Aaroh The ascending scale or pattern of notes in a raga.
Avroh The descending scale or pattern of notes in a raga.
Pakad A catchphrase or signature musical motif that instantly identifies the raga.
Swara A musical note. S, R, G, m, P, D, N correspond roughly to Do, Re, Mi, Fa, Sol, La, Ti.
Thaat The parent scale system of Hindustani music, used to classify ragas.
